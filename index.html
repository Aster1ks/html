Moluccan Cockatoo Care, Feeding and Behavior

Freshwater/pellets/fresh produce daily, large-sized cage, many wood toys for chewing and destroying, exposure to radio or television when alone to keep company, a significant amount of time out of cage daily to socialize and be handled, annual veterinary check-ups, regular nail clipping +/- misting, best housed singly.
